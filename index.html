About Me

As a seasoned IT professional with over 20 years of experience in people and project management, I specialize in driving operational efficiency through innovative change management strategies.

Holding a PhD in Change Management in IT organizations, I pioneered a new model tailored to the software product life cycle stages. My certifications in ITIL, TOGAF, and Agile/Lean methodologies, combined with a strategic application of Scrum, Kanban, Waterfall, OKRs, Scrum-of-Scrums, BABOK, and PMBOK, reflect my holistic approach to aligning IT processes with organizational goals.

My expertise extends to ERP, Warehouse Management, Supply Chain, Master Data, Inventory Management, Fulfillment, eCommerce, iGaming, Crypto & fiat payments, blockchain, KYC & AML, data integrations, and analytics. This comprehensive background enables me to deliver impactful solutions across diverse business domains.
